What we do

Running a charity or social enterprise is hard. The hours are long, the pay is low and sometimes its hard to find anyone to say thanks!....But what makes it even harder is the reporting. You need to get data for board papers...stats for funding reports and find ways of letting your supporters know how things are going. You dig through dozens of spreadsheets, multiple versions of files and the one time you miss a Tweet all hell breaks loose....sound familiar? It doesn't have to.

At Change I.T. we help not for profits move into the 21st century. We map your reporting requirements and get key information automatically sent to you each week, month or whenever you want. We can build you cloud based intranets so you can collaborate on documents with colleagues and access them from your office, home or phone!

We can build beautiful responsive websites with calendars, social media and booking pages integrated in, and we can help you plan and schedule your social media so you're never left wondering "Did anyone remember to Tweet this?"

We only work not for profits and keep our costs low by adapting existing technology and using developers and designers from across the world. So if you don't like your I.T.

We can Change I.T!
